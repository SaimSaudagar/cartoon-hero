Sung Jinwoo

About Sung Jinwoo

Sung Jinwoo is the main protagonist of Solo Leveling. Originally an infamously weak E-Rank Hunter, he got the chance of a lifetime when he was selected as the Player of the System, giving him the unique ability to grow in strength without restraint. As a result, by taking advantage of the opportunities offered to him by the System, Jinwoo eventually rose to power as humanity's greatest hunter and the second Shadow Monarch.

Sung Jin Woo and his army

Sung Jin Woo's powers

  1. Kasaka's Venom Fang: A dagger made from Kasaka's Venom Fang. Has two effects: Paralyze and Bleed.
  2. Knight Killer: A dagger designed to fight against armored enemies. Deals an extra +25% damage against armored opponents.
  3. Baruka's Dagger: A dagger once used by the elf warlord Baruka.
  4. Demon King's Daggers: A pair of daggers once used by the Demon King Baran. Has a second effect called Two as One, which grants bonus attack power to each dagger based on the user's strength stat.
  5. Demon King's Longsword: A longsword once used by Baran. Has a second effect called Storm of White Flames, which summons a small lightning storm within a certain area.
  6. Kamish's Wrath: A pair of daggers forged from the fang of the dragon Kamish. They are mana-sensitive, meaning that their power aligns with their user's strength stat, and also allow their user to alter their weight however they please.
